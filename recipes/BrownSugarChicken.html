Ingredients:

  • 3tsp brown sugar
  • 1.5tsp smoked or sweet paprika
  • 1tsp chili powder or 0.5tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1tsp dried oregano
  • 1tsp dried thyme
  • 1.5tsp salt
  • 1tsp garlic powder
  • 0.5tsp onion powder
  • o.25tsp black pepper

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 425f
  2. Pound each chicken breast to approx. 0.8 inch at thickest part
  3. Combine seasonings
  4. Place chicken in pan or on baking sheet. Drizzle with oil, then rub seasoning into chicken
  5. Bake in oven for 14 min, then broil on high heat for 2 min or until cooked through. Chicken should be golden with crisp edges
  6. Remove chicken and let sit for 5 min, then serve
