Ingredients:

  • 2 medium russet potatoes, shredded
  • 0.5 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 0.25 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup oil
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Rinse shredded potatoes until water is clear, then squeeze dry
  2. Place in a bowl and mix in onion, flour, and egg
  3. Heat about 0.25 inch of oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. When sizzling, place potatoes in pan in a 0.5 inch layer
  4. Cook until brownedon bottom and flip and brown other side
  5. Remove from pan and drain on paper towels
  6. Season and serve
